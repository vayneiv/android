HELP MENU
---------

Two options to start OCR. Either click CAPTURE PHOTO or CHOOSE PHOTO. 

If CAPTURE PHOTO is pressed, camera app will open.

If CHOOSE PHOTO is pressed, photo library will open.

After Taking or choosing a photo of preferred text. Wait for a moment and OCRed text will appear in the TEXT AREA. 

If you want to save, press SAVE button. Enter filename, and fle type. Either .doc, .rtf or  .text.
